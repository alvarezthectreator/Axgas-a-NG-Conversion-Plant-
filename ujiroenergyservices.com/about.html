About AXGAS

AXGAS is a Lagos-based accredited conversion center specializing in vehicle conversions to Compressed Natural Gas (CNG). With over 300 successful conversions completed, we are committed to providing sustainable and cost-effective fuel solutions.

Who We Are

Your Trusted Partner for CNG Vehicle Conversion

AXGAS is more than a conversion center. We are a strategic partner for fleet owners, transport operators, businesses, and individual drivers looking to switch to cleaner, more affordable, and more reliable fuel. Our team combines technical precision, certified processes, and practical local expertise to deliver safe CNG conversions that perform in real-world Nigerian driving conditions.

  • Petrol-to-CNG conversion for passenger and light commercial vehicles
  • Diesel-to-CNG dual-fuel systems for logistics and heavy-duty fleets
  • Fleet conversion planning with ROI analysis and rollout support
  • Scheduled maintenance, diagnostics, recalibration, and technical support
  • Safety, compliance, and certification aligned with national standards

With active conversion centers in Victoria Island, Orile, and Trans Amadi, plus partnerships with Toyota Nigeria, Lotus Bank, PCNGI, and other key stakeholders, AXGAS is helping accelerate Nigeria's transition to cleaner mobility. Every project is handled with a focus on measurable savings, safety, compliance, and long-term operating value.
