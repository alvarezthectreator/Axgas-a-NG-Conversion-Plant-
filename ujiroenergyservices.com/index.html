Logistics Fleet Diesel-to-CNG

Challenge

A logistics firm with high diesel consumption across a truck fleet facing unsustainable OPEX costs and pressure to improve ESG performance.

Solution

Diesel-CNG dual fuel system installed across the fleet, with CNG partially replacing diesel during combustion while retaining the diesel ignition system.

  • ✓ 30–40% diesel displacement per vehicle
  • ✓ Improved ESG & CSR performance
